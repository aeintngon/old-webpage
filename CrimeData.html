Data visualization to compare crime statistics in DC and Chicago

July 18, 2017

In 2008, the District of Columbia Metropolitan Police Department reported a total of 34,205 crime incidents and in 2016, 37,186 crime incidents were reported. The bar plot below also shows that the crime incidents have been increasing steadily since 2009 with a peak in 2014.

Count of crime incidents from 2008-2017

In 2008, Chicago Police Department's CLEAR (Citizen Law Enforcement Analysis and Reporting) system reported a total of 426,796 crime incidents and in 2016, a total of 267,626 crime incidents were reported. According to the plot below, the total number of crime incidents has been decreasing steadily since 2008.
